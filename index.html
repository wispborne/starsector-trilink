download TriLink

Generate a one-click link that installs your mod through TriOS

  1. Add your .version url. Paste the link to your mod's .version file with a working directDownloadURL. Don't have one? Paste a link to your mod's .zip download instead.
  2. Mod ID (optional). From your mod_info.json. Lets TriOS skip the mod download if it's already installed.
  3. Dependencies (optional). Click Add dependency and paste each required mod's .version or .zip link, and ideally the mod id.
  4. Copy & paste. Click Copy, and paste it into your forum post. Test it out! Requires TriOS 1.6.0 or later.
  5. Test it! Requires TriOS 1.6.0 or later.

lightbulb A .version file URL is better than a mod download link; it updates whenever you update your version file and helps avoid unnecessary redownloads.

lightbulb The optional Mod ID (from mod_info.json) combined with a .version file lets TriOS skip a mod that's already installed.
